Our Mission

The UMass Lowell AI & ML Club is a student-led organization dedicated to fostering curiosity, collaboration, and innovation in artificial intelligence and machine learning. We bring together students from all disciplines to learn, build, and explore the technologies shaping our future.

Whether you're a seasoned researcher or just getting started, our workshops, hackathons, and project teams offer something for everyone.

Hands-On Workshops

Weekly sessions on deep learning, NLP, computer vision, and more — guided by experienced members.

Collaborative Projects

Work in teams to build real-world AI applications — from chatbots to predictive models.

Industry Connections

Guest speakers, company visits, and networking events with AI professionals in the Greater Boston area.
